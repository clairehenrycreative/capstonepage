A Visual Brand Identity for a Community-Centered Sustainable Marketplace

Claire Henry - MAGWD Capstone Project

Project Overview

Hook: Superette merges sustainable commerce with community to uplift Kansas City neighborhoods and global environmental health.

Problem Statement: In 2025, finding products that truly align with your values and belief system has become increasingly challenging. Superette addresses this by creating a brick-and-mortar store centered around thoughtful shopping and genuine community-based connection.
